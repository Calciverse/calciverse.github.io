What is Percentage?

Percentage is a way to express a number as a fraction of 100. It is widely used in finance, business, and everyday calculations.

How Percentage is Calculated?

To calculate percentage, multiply the value by the percentage and divide by 100.

Formula

Result = (Value × Percentage) / 100

Example

If you want to find 20% of ₹500, the result will be ₹100.

Uses of Percentage

  • Discount calculations
  • Profit & loss
  • Marks and grading
  • Financial analysis
