What is Age Calculator?

An age calculator helps you determine your exact age from your date of birth to the current date.

How Age is Calculated?

It calculates the difference between your birth date and the current date in years, months and days.

Why Use Age Calculator?

  • Know your exact age
  • Useful for forms and documents
  • Quick and accurate results
