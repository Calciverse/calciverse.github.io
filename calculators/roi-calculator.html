ROI Calculator

Calculate return on investment, profit and ROI percentage instantly.

What is ROI?

ROI (Return on Investment) measures the profitability of an investment. It shows how much profit you gain relative to your initial investment.

How ROI is Calculated?

ROI is calculated using the formula:

ROI (%) = [(Return - Investment) / Investment] × 100

Example

If you invest ₹10,000 and get ₹15,000 return, your profit is ₹5,000 and ROI is 50%.

Why ROI is Important?

  • Measure investment performance
  • Compare different investments
  • Make better financial decisions
