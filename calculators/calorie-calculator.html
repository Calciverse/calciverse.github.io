Estimate your daily calorie needs based on your body and lifestyle.

What is a Calorie Calculator?

This calculator helps estimate how many calories your body needs each day to maintain, lose or gain weight.

How Calories are Calculated?

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor formula to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), then multiply it with activity level.

Why Calories Matter?

  • Maintain healthy weight
  • Improve fitness
  • Build muscle or lose fat
  • Track daily nutrition

Tips for Better Results

  • Be consistent with your activity level
  • Track calories daily
  • Combine diet with exercise
