What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax applied on the supply of goods and services. It has replaced multiple taxes in India.

How GST is Calculated?

GST is calculated based on the price of goods and applicable GST rate.

GST Formula

GST Amount = (Original Price × GST Rate) / 100

Example

If a product costs ₹1000 and GST is 18%, then GST = ₹180 and total price = ₹1180.
