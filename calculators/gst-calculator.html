What is GST Calculator?

GST Calculator is an online financial tool that helps you calculate Goods and Services Tax in India. It allows you to add GST to a base amount or remove GST from a gross amount instantly.

GST Slabs in India

India follows GST slabs of 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%. Most goods and services fall under the 18% slab.

Formula Used

Adding GST: GST = Amount × Rate / 100

Removing GST: Base = Gross / (1 + Rate/100)
