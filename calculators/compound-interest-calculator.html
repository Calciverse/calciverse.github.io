What is Compound Interest?

Compound interest is interest calculated on the initial principal and also on the accumulated interest of previous periods. It accelerates investment growth over time.

Compound Interest Formula

A = P (1 + r/n)nt

Why Compound Interest is Powerful?

  • Wealth grows exponentially
  • Ideal for long-term investments
  • Maximizes returns through reinvestment
