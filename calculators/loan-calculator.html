What is Loan Calculator?

Loan Calculator is a free online financial tool that helps you calculate monthly EMI, total interest payable and full repayment schedule. It is useful for personal loans, car loans, home loans and business loans.

How Loan EMI is Calculated?

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] ÷ [(1+R)^N – 1]

P = Loan Amount | R = Monthly Interest Rate | N = Total Months

Benefits of Using Online Loan Calculator

  • Instant EMI estimation
  • Accurate total interest calculation
  • Better loan comparison
  • Financial planning support
