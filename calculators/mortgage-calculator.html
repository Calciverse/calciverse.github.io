What is Mortgage Calculator?

A Mortgage Calculator is an online financial tool that helps you estimate your monthly home loan EMI (Equated Monthly Installment). It calculates how much you need to pay every month based on home price, down payment, interest rate and loan tenure.

How Does Mortgage EMI Work?

Mortgage EMI is calculated using the standard amortization formula. Each EMI consists of two parts:

  • Principal Amount – Portion of loan repaid
  • Interest Amount – Cost of borrowing

Mortgage EMI Formula

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] ÷ [(1+R)^N – 1]

Where:
P = Loan Amount
R = Monthly Interest Rate
N = Total number of months

Benefits of Using Online Mortgage Calculator

  • Instant EMI calculation
  • Full amortization schedule
  • Compare loan options easily
  • Better financial planning
