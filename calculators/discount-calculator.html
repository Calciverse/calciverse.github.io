Discount Calculator

Calculate discount amount, final price and savings instantly.

What is Discount?

A discount is a reduction in the original price of a product or service. It is commonly used in sales, promotions and offers.

How Discount is Calculated?

Discount is calculated by multiplying the original price by the discount percentage.

Formula

Discount = (Price × Discount%) / 100

Final Price = Price - Discount

Example

If a product costs ₹1000 and has a 20% discount, the discount is ₹200 and final price is ₹800.
