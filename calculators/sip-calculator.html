What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows investors to invest a fixed amount monthly in mutual funds. It benefits from rupee cost averaging and compounding growth.

SIP Formula

M = P × ((1 + r)^n − 1) × (1 + r) / r

Benefits of SIP Investment

  • Disciplined investing
  • Compounding returns
  • Long-term wealth creation
