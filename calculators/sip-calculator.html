What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It helps in disciplined investing and wealth creation over time.

How SIP Returns are Calculated?

SIP returns are calculated using compound interest formula considering monthly investments and expected returns.

SIP Formula

M = P × [(1 + r)^n - 1] / r × (1 + r)

Example

If you invest ₹5,000 monthly at 12% annual return for 5 years, your investment grows significantly due to compounding.

Benefits of SIP

  • Disciplined investing
  • Power of compounding
  • Flexible investment
  • Long-term wealth creation
