EMI Calculator

Calculate EMI, total interest and payment instantly.

What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed monthly payment made towards loan repayment.

How EMI is Calculated?

EMI depends on loan amount, interest rate and tenure.

Formula

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N – 1]
