What is Fixed Deposit (FD)?

Fixed Deposit is a safe investment option where you deposit a fixed amount for a specific period and earn interest on it.

How FD Interest is Calculated?

FD interest is calculated based on compound interest formula depending on compounding frequency.

FD Formula

A = P (1 + r/n)^(n × t)

Example

If you invest ₹50,000 at 7% interest for 3 years, your maturity value will increase based on compounding.

Benefits of FD

  • Safe investment
  • Guaranteed returns
  • Flexible tenure
