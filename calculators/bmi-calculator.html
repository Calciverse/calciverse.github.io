What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure used to determine whether a person has a healthy body weight based on height and weight.

How BMI is Calculated?

BMI is calculated by dividing weight (kg) by the square of height (meters).

Formula

BMI = Weight (kg) / Height² (m)

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: Less than 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30+

Why BMI is Important?

BMI helps identify whether you are at risk of health issues related to underweight or overweight conditions.
