Ideal Weight Calculator

Find your ideal body weight based on your height and gender.

What is Ideal Weight?

Ideal weight is an estimate of the healthiest body weight based on your height and gender. It helps you maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

How Ideal Weight is Calculated?

This calculator uses the Devine formula, which is widely accepted for estimating healthy body weight.

Formula

Male: 50 + 0.9 × (height − 152)

Female: 45.5 + 0.9 × (height − 152)

Why Ideal Weight Matters?

  • Better overall health
  • Reduced disease risk
  • Improved fitness
  • Balanced lifestyle

Tips to Maintain Ideal Weight

  • Eat balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Stay consistent
