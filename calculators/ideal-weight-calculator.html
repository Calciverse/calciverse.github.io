Ideal Weight Calculator

Find your ideal body weight based on your height and gender.

What is Ideal Weight?

Ideal body weight is an estimate of the healthiest weight based on height and gender. It helps maintain overall health and fitness.

How is Ideal Weight Calculated?

This calculator uses the Devine formula to estimate ideal body weight.

Formula

Male: 50 + 0.9 × (height in cm − 152)

Female: 45.5 + 0.9 × (height in cm − 152)

Why Ideal Weight Matters?

  • Better health
  • Reduced disease risk
  • Improved fitness
  • Balanced lifestyle
