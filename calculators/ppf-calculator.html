What is Public Provident Fund (PPF)?

PPF is a long-term government-backed savings scheme in India offering safe and tax-efficient returns. It is ideal for long-term wealth creation and retirement planning.

How PPF Interest is Calculated?

PPF interest is compounded annually. The government announces interest rates quarterly. This calculator estimates maturity based on yearly compounding.

Benefits of PPF Investment

  • Government-backed security
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C
  • Long-term compounding growth
  • Guaranteed returns
